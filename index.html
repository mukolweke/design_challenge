About Society for
Conversion GIS Membership

If you are a GIS professional or are interested in using GIS for conservation, you can become member of SCGIS! The diverse membership includes members from many different countries in the world. Join this expanding network today!

Benefits

As an SCGIS member, you can get access to a large and expanding network of conservation GIS professionals worldwide. And, you can meet many of your fellow members at the SCGIS annual conference in California! When you become a member, you can:

  • Be part of a dynamic community of passionate GIS conservationist that motivate and support each other. Engage in discussion, seek advice and get GIS technical support from other members on our website and listserv.
  • Access years of knowledge from past listserv email conversations via our listserv archive. Get discounted registration for the SCGIS annual conference.
  • Attend free webinar offered to share technical and conservation knowledge with SCGIS members around the world.
  • Create a member profile, browse other members profiles, and send private messeges to other members.
  • Post descriptions of the GIS projects that you are working on.
  • Create or join SCGIS chapter.
  • Browse and post GIS-related jobs on the jobs board. Post GIS-related news and events.
